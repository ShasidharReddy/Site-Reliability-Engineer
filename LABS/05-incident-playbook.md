Lab 05 — Incident playbook exercises

1) Triage sequence:
- Observe: check dashboards, alert details
- Scope: which services/regions are impacted
- Mitigate: scale, restart, route around faulty nodes
- RCA: collect logs/traces, identify root cause
- Postmortem: document timeline, impact, action items

2) Practice exercise: simulate pod OOM or high CPU and follow runbook to mitigate and resolve.
